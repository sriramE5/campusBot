University Rules (sample):

1. Quiet hours: From 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM daily, students must maintain low noise levels in dormitories.
2. Parking: Students must register their vehicles at the campus security office. Unauthorized vehicles may be towed.
3. Academic integrity: Cheating, plagiarism, and aiding cheating are strictly prohibited. Violations will result in disciplinary action.
4. Library behaviour: No food or drink in the reading rooms. Laptops are allowed but must be muted.
5. Guest policy: Residents may have guests until 11 PM; overnight guests must be registered at the hostel office.
